Surfstick and internet sharing with networkmanager

04 Jul 2016
software / network / linux

Wired internet broke after floodings. To bridge the time till all repairs are finished I received a K4203 SurfStick. Some instructions how to get it to work and share the internet connection follows. #K4203 SurfStick This stick provides a wireless internet connection through UMTS. In my case it didn’t work out of the box. Upon insertion in an USB slot it registered itself as a CD-reader. Apparently USB devices can have different modes of operation and change according to the situation.
