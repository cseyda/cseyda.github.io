Bigger, Leaner, Stronger

03 Nov 2017
review / notes

by Michael Matthews (goodreads link)

Good overview over the main fitness topics:

  • mental condition, willpower, social pressure
  • eating (habits), calories, macros
  • general concepts of a training plan for hobby lifter
  • compound movements: squat, deadlift, benchpress, overhead press. a little short, but as an introduction it should be enough. links to online videos are given.
  • supplement overview. what helps, what not, in which doses.

overall good introductions without much depths. not much to learn for me unfortunately.

rating: 3⁄5
